Film Reviews

Bahubali: The Beginning

Rating: 8/10

Cast

  • Prabhas as Shivudu / Baahubali
  • Rana Daggubati as Bhallaladeva
  • Anushka Shetty as Devasena
  • Tamannaah as Avanthika

Crew

  • Directed by S.S. Rajamouli
  • Produced by Shobu Yarlagadda, Prasad Devineni
  • Music by M.M. Keeravani
  • Cinematography K.K. Senthil Kumar

Story

"Bahubali: The Beginning" is an epic tale of valor, betrayal, and destiny. The story follows Shivudu, a young man who discovers his true identity as Baahubali, the rightful heir to the throne of Mahishmati. Guided by his curiosity and thirst for justice, Baahubali embarks on a journey to reclaim his kingdom from the ruthless clutches of his power-hungry cousin, Bhallaladeva. The narrative is replete with breathtaking battles, political intrigue, and heartwrenching emotions that keep the audience hooked from start to finish. As Baahubali uncovers the secrets of his past and learns about the love story between his parents, Amarendra Baahubali and Devasena, the audience is taken on an unforgettable adventure filled with stunning visuals and awe-inspiring set pieces.

Highlights

"Bahubali: The Beginning" excels in its grand storytelling and visual spectacle. The intricate world-building, majestic palaces, and larger-than-life characters create an immersive cinematic experience. Prabhas shines as both Shivudu and Baahubali, commanding the screen with his powerful presence. Rana Daggubati's portrayal of the antagonist Bhallaladeva adds depth and intensity to the narrative. The film's music by M.M. Keeravani and Rajamouli's masterful direction contribute to its epic feel.

Final Verdict

"Bahubali: The Beginning" is a monumental achievement in Indian cinema. With its captivating story, stunning visuals, and strong performances, the film sets a new benchmark for epic storytelling. While the film's runtime might feel long, the grandeur and emotional depth make it a must-watch for all movie enthusiasts. Rajamouli's vision and execution have created a cinematic masterpiece that will be remembered for generations to come.
